About

Who is behind the camera.

Hi, We are Mononata Team — focus on visual storyteller based in Denpasar, Bali. We help brands, couples, and creators turn fleeting moments into lasting visuals.

Over the years We have collaborated with local and international brands, villa owners, event organizers, and couples across Bali. Whether it's a product shoot, a wedding film, or a brand reel, our goal stays the same — capture the story in a way that looks real and feels honest.
