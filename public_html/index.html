About

Who is behind the camera.

Hi, we are the Mononata team — visual storytellers based in Denpasar, Bali. We help brands, couples, and creators turn fleeting moments into lasting visuals.

Over the years we have collaborated with local and international brands, villa owners, event organizers, and couples across Bali. Whether it's a product shoot, a wedding film, or a brand reel, our goal stays the same — capture the story in a way that looks real and feels honest.

FAQ

Quick answers before you reach out.

Do you travel outside Bali for shoots?

Yes — we cover all of Bali and regularly travel to Lombok, Nusa Penida, the Gili Islands, Java, and beyond. Travel costs are billed at cost.

How long until we receive the final photos and videos?

Photos: typically 7–14 days. Short-form reels: 3–7 days. Wedding films and brand films: 3–6 weeks depending on scope. Rush delivery is available on request.

Do you provide RAW files?

We deliver fully edited, color-graded files as standard. RAW or unedited files can be provided on request and are quoted separately.

What is your booking process?

Send us a brief with the project type, location, date, duration, and references. We reply within 24 hours with a quote. To lock the date we ask for a 30% deposit, with the remainder due on delivery.

Do you offer drone footage?

Yes. Drone coverage is included in most video packages and can be added to any photo session, subject to local flight regulations.

What payment methods do you accept?

Bank transfer (IDR), Wise, and PayPal are all accepted. Invoices are provided for every project.
